Student life

At Msambweni Technical and Vocational College, we foster a dynamic and fulfilling student life, recognizing its significance in holistic development. Our college abounds with an array of activities, clubs, and services designed to enrich your college experience. Embrace the vibrant Msambweni Technical and Vocational College community and explore diverse opportunities that contribute to your personal growth and academic journey.

Sports

Msambweni Technical and Vocational College boasts of spirited games of football, basketball, volleyball, and more on our spacious sports field. Channel your competitive spirit through friendly yet spirited Inter-Departmental sports competitions within the college community.

Participate in tournaments and matches that promote teamwork, sportsmanship, and healthy competition. Join the thrill of sports events and championships organized by Msambweni Technical and Vocational College.

WELCOME MESSAGE

Hello and Welcome to Msambweni Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) institution. MTVC was established in September 2019 in conformity with the TVET Act No. 29 of 2013 as a Centre of Excellence in Electrical & Electronics Engineering with state-of-the-art equipment. Our mission is to provide quality technical and vocational education that empowers youth with relevant skills for industry and entrepreneurship. Join us and be part of a transformative learning experience.

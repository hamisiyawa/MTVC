WELCOME MESSAGE

Hello and Welcome to Msambweni Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) institution. MTVC was established in September 2019 in conformity with the TVET Act No. 29 of 2013 as a Centre of Excellence in Electrical & Electronics Engineering with state-of-the-art equipment. Our mission is to provide quality technical and vocational education that empowers youth with relevant skills for industry and entrepreneurship. Join us and be part of a transformative learning experience.

College Departments

Computing and Informatics

The Department provides trainees with practical, hands-on training in computing, information technology, and related disciplines.

Business Studies

Provides specialized training in various aspects of business, equipping students with practical and professional skills.

Fashion Design

Dedicated to nurturing creativity and innovation in aspiring fashion designers through comprehensive training.

Electrical & Electronic Engineering

The Department is divided into Power and Electronics and prides itself on its modern equipment and workshops.

Mechanical Engineering

Focuses on the principles of engineering, physics, and materials science applied to the design and manufacturing of machines.

Tourism and Hospitality

Equips students with skills essential for the Tourism and Hospitality industry, with an emphasis on professionalism and customer service.
